Roman Zuzuk is a master of primitive abstraction whose bold, vibrant palette and expressive figures capture the raw essence of human emotion and experience.

Working primarily in oil on canvas and burlap, as well as the ancient medium of encaustic, Zuzuk creates works that bridge the gap between folk art traditions and contemporary expression. His distinctive style features simplified forms, rich textural surfaces, and a fearless use of color that brings joy and vitality to each composition.
